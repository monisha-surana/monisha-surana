About Us

Incorporated in the year of 2009, VARDHMAN UDYOG is one of the principal manufacturer and supplier of Moth (Matki, Turkish gram, Dew bean), Moth dal Khadda, Moth dal from Nokha, Rajasthan, India. Vardhman Udyog has successfully established itself as a leading pulses dealer in the market. It deals in the manufacturing & processing of Moth (Matki), Moth Dal Khadda, Moth dal. The above products are available in the brand name of Vardhman Udyog, Vardhman, Angoor, Double Horse, Ganpati etc. in packing of 30 Kg & 50 Kg. The company is flourishing well under the guidance of the mentors, Mr. Sunil Surana, Mr. Surendra Surana (Owner) and Mr. Mahender Kumar Surana (CEO). The organization is located in Rajasthan (India).
